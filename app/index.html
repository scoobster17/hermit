Welcome to Hermit

This is the hermit app. In a great juxtaposition of the way the hermit crab picks many empty seashells to wrap around itself, this app is a 'shell' to all of your many web apps that you don't want clogging up your browser tabs.

Add a new tab

Here you can specify any* site which will be added as a tab in your app.

Tab details

This shows in the tab at the top of the screen. For example, look at this tab, next to the Hermit icon it says 'Hermit'. Required if a logo is not present.

Configuration

If you have specified a name in the above box, you can toggle whether it is shown or not here. Doing this saves the previous value so you don't need to remember what you had there before if you keep changing your mind!
